# STAR+R Story Bank -- Anthony Cannon

Stories prepared for interview use. Each follows STAR+R format:
- **S**ituation -- context
- **T**ask -- what was required
- **A**ction -- what AC did specifically
- **R**esult -- measurable outcome
- **R**eflection -- what was learned, what would change

---

## 1. Scaling the AI Community (Team Leadership)

- **S:** Accenture had no organized AI practitioner network. Senior leaders wanted adoption but no structure existed.
- **T:** Build a community that would drive real AI adoption across the firm.
- **A:** Founded with 10 members. Designed engagement model, curated content, ran enablement sessions, created recognition mechanisms.
- **R:** Grew from 10 to 600+ active contributors. Became go-to resource for AI adoption firm-wide.
- **Reflection:** Building a community is team-building without formal authority. Key was making participation valuable to members. Would formalize mentorship tracks earlier.

**Tags:** team-leadership, enablement, scaling, community

## 2. $100M+ Commercial Expansion (Strategic Partnership)

- **S:** Global consumer/retail client had a $10M flagship program. AC was senior advisor.
- **T:** Establish delivery credibility and executive trust to unlock enterprise-wide expansion.
- **A:** Personally designed 29-step operating model. Delivered results C-suite leaders could point to. Built relationships across Chief Data/AI, Global Technology, Business Services.
- **R:** Enabled $100M+ in downstream commercial expansion.

**Tags:** commercial-expansion, executive-relationships, enterprise, trust

## 3. 5,000-Analyst Enablement Program (Partner Enablement)

- **S:** Accenture needed to upskill thousands of analysts on AI-enabled delivery methods.
- **T:** Design enablement that changes how people work, not just checks a training box.
- **A:** Co-designed applied AI enablement programs focused on production delivery workflows. Made it hands-on.
- **R:** 5,000+ analysts across North America enabled. Programs referenced by leadership as models for AI adoption.
- **Reflection:** Scale requires simplification. Teach 3 things that matter most and make them easy to adopt. Same principle for partner enablement.

**Tags:** enablement, scale, training, AI-adoption

## 4. ProcessOS -- Six-Agent Cascade (Joint Solution Development)

- **S:** Client needed enterprise process design capability that could scale across markets and workstreams.
- **T:** Build a reusable, referenceable architecture for process design using AI agents.
- **A:** Designed six-agent cascade producing tiered deliverables from interaction models to decision-rights frameworks. Built as reference architecture.
- **R:** Deployed on active client delivery. Became repeatable pattern for process design engagements.
- **Reflection:** Reference architectures only work if simple enough for others to adopt. Hard part is documenting so a partner team can deploy without you.

**Tags:** architecture, multi-agent, reference-pattern, reusability

## 5. California Vaccine Platform -- 8-Week Sprint (Speed + Stakes)

- **S:** California needed statewide vaccine management platform during peak COVID demand. No existing infrastructure.
- **T:** Onboard 950+ provider organizations across 58 counties in 8 weeks.
- **A:** Led awareness and adoption program. Established partnerships with payers, community partners, government agencies. Delivered personalized onboarding at scale.

## 7. Change Digital Twin -- Behavioral Prediction (Technical Depth)

- **S:** Needed to predict organizational adoption patterns for enterprise transformation programs.
- **T:** Build a system using rigorous experimental methodology, not gut feel.
- **A:** Applied counterbalanced designs, calibrated baselines, Cramer's V quality gates (<=0.20). Built behavioral prediction platform.
- **R:** Validated 6-10 percentage point impact on adoption behaviors across controlled experiments.
- **Reflection:** Enterprise AI is about methodology around the model. Quality gates and experimental rigor separate production systems from demos.

**Tags:** experimental-design, prediction, quality-gates, enterprise-AI
