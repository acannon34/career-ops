## C) Level Strategy

### Level Analysis

| Dimension | JD Level | AC Natural Level |
|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| Title | Manager | Managing Director / VP-equivalent |
| Scope | Build + lead a team of Partner SAs | Has led 70-person cross-functional orgs, $135M+ in programs |
| Decision authority | Co-build partner strategy with GTM leadership | Currently the strategy authority for enterprise transformations |

AC is naturally one level above this role. The JD says "Manager" but the responsibilities (senior technical thought partner, represent Anthropic on most important partnerships, co-build partner strategy) read closer to Senior Manager or Director scope.

### Plan: "Sell senior without lying"

1. Lead with builder credibility: "I've shipped production systems on your API. I don't just talk about Claude -- I build with it."
2. Frame Accenture MD as proof of partner DNA: "I know how GSIs think, sell, staff, and deliver because I ran it from the inside at the most senior level."
3. Position community scaling as team-building proof: "Grew an AI practice from 10 to 600+ -- that's the same muscle as building a partner SA team from scratch."
4. Use $100M+ expansion number to signal commercial impact without claiming a sales title.

### Plan: "If downleveled"

- The posted comp ($315K-$380K base) is competitive. If TC with equity lands in the $500K-$600K+ range, the title is secondary.
- Negotiate: written 6-month review with clear promotion criteria to Senior Manager or Director.
- Ask: "What does the next 18 months look like for this team's scope?" Signal that you expect to grow the function, not just fill a seat.
