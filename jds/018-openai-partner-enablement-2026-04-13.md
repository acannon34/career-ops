## About the Team

OpenAI's Go-To-Market organization supports customers in deploying AI products across their businesses. The Partnerships team manages a strategic global ecosystem of partners designed to accelerate customer success, enable responsible AI adoption, and drive growth. Partnerships span technology partners, systems integrators, and strategic collaborators.

## About the Role

This leadership position sits within GTM Partnerships Enablement, focused specifically on OpenAI's strategic AWS Cloud partnership. The role combines strategy development, program design, and operational execution to equip partners with knowledge, tools, and resources for successful implementations. As a people manager, you will lead and grow a team developing scalable enablement programs for the expanding partner ecosystem.

You will collaborate with Partnerships leadership, Partner Directors, Partner Technical Success teams, and AWS stakeholders to design initiatives improving partner readiness and accelerating joint customer outcomes.

## Key Responsibilities

- Build and lead the Partner Enablement function managing teams focused on scaling the AWS Cloud partnership ecosystem
- Define and execute enablement strategy providing tools, training, and programs for cloud-native solution delivery
- Design onboarding and certification programs accelerating partner ramp with AWS-aligned deployment models
- Develop scalable playbooks, training content for sales and technical teams emphasizing AWS GTM and co-sell motions
- Partner with leadership and AWS stakeholders aligning enablement priorities with joint strategic goals
- Collaborate with Partner Directors driving partner performance and pipeline across AWS opportunities
- Support Partner Technical Success tracking enablement impact through metrics and dashboards
- Translate product capabilities into partner-ready messaging and collateral aligned to AWS workflows
- Build and maintain centralized enablement resource hubs including AWS-specific materials
- Capture partner feedback and field insights informing broader GTM strategy

## Required Qualifications

- Experience building and leading partner enablement and scaled programs in high-growth technology companies
- Track record managing and developing teams responsible for enablement, partner programs, or GTM strategy
- Strong program management and operational leadership skills driving cross-functional initiatives across complex organizations
- Experience with partner ecosystems including systems integrators, consulting firms, hyperscalers, and technology partners
- Comfortable collaborating across Partnerships, Sales, Product, Marketing, and Technical functions

- Strong interest in AI landscape and emerging technologies with ability translating complex technology into actionable partner programs
- Ability thriving in fast-paced environments with high ambiguity building new systems and programs from ground up
