## Role Overview

Partner Sales Manager, Systems Integrators. Sits at the intersection of partnerships and sales. Manages a global and regional portfolio of Systems Integrator partners while driving associated revenue. Reports to Head of Partner Sales.

**Location:** San Francisco, CA | New York City, NY
**Compensation:** $300,000 - $355,000 USD (OTE for sales role)
**Work Arrangement:** Hybrid, minimum 25% office time

## Core Responsibilities

- Coordinate directly with Sales Leaders, Account Executives, and Solutions Architects to integrate partners into sales cycles at optimal moments while maintaining clear accountability
- Develop multi-level relationships across partner organizations, from delivery teams to alliance executives, serving as primary Anthropic contact
- Own commercial relationships with assigned SI partners, driving partner-sourced and influenced revenue against targets
- Create and execute joint go-to-market plans including account mapping, pipeline generation, and co-sell activities
- Ensure partners receive training, certification, and positioning materials for Claude
- Monitor pipeline health, forecast partner-attached revenue, and escalate blockers to cross-functional teams
- Gather market feedback from partner interactions and inform product and go-to-market strategy
- Contribute to developing partner sales processes, playbooks, and scaling best practices

## Required Qualifications

- 7+ years in partner sales, channel sales, alliances, business development, or direct sales roles within technology companies where partners are heavily involved
- Demonstrated track record driving revenue through partners with specific examples of built pipeline, influenced deals, and lasting relationships
- Strong commercial instincts including co-sell agreement structuring and multi-party deal navigation
- Experience in early-stage or high-growth environments where processes require building
- Excellent cross-level communication and relationship-building abilities
- Collaborative mindset energized by cross-functional work
- Comfort with ambiguity and ability to create structure where none exists
- Willingness to travel supporting partner relationships and customer engagements
- Genuine interest in AI and commitment to safe, responsible development

## Preferred Qualifications

- Prior experience at major Systems Integrators or global consulting firms
- Background in AI, cloud platforms, developer tools, or similar categories emphasizing technical enablement
- Familiarity with consumption-based or API-first business models and partner economics
- Multi-geography partner relationship management experience
- History as early partner sales function member who helped scaling efforts
